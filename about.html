About Cork Solar & Electrical

Our Story

Who We Are

Cork Solar & Electrical is a Cork-based electrical contractor serving homeowners and businesses across Cork City and County Cork. Founded by David O'Leary, the company has been trading for 3 years and has grown to a team of 7 — including 6 qualified electricians and office administrator Marion.

We cover the full range of residential and commercial electrical services — from house rewires and fuse board upgrades to office fit-outs, solar installations and emergency callouts. We are SEAI approved, Safe Electric registered and fully insured on every job.

Our reputation has been built almost entirely on word of mouth and referrals. That says everything about how we approach our work — with care, professionalism and honest pricing on every job, no matter how big or small.

Our Accreditations

Cork Solar & Electrical holds all required accreditations to carry out electrical and solar work across Ireland.

SEAI Approved Installer

Registered with the Sustainable Energy Authority of Ireland. Customers qualify for SEAI grants on solar panel and EV charger installations.

Safe Electric Registered

Registered with Safe Electric — Ireland's national register of competent electricians. All work is certified and compliant with ETCI wiring regulations.

RECI Registered

Members of the Register of Electrical Contractors of Ireland. RECI membership guarantees a high standard of electrical workmanship and safety.
