Who We Are

Cork Solar & Electrical is a residential and commercial electrical contractor based in Cork, Ireland. We are registered as SEAI approved electricians.

Address: Railway Cottage, Ballinamona, Mourneabbey, Mallow, Co. Cork
Email: info@csae.ie
Phone: 085 718 8103

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us through our website contact forms. This includes your name, phone number, email address, address, and project details.

We may also collect information automatically through cookies and website analytics tools when you browse our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To respond to your enquiries and provide quotes for our services. To follow up on submitted forms via phone, email or WhatsApp. To manage leads through our CRM system (GoHighLevel). To improve our website and the services we offer. To run targeted advertising campaigns on Google and Facebook/Meta.

GoHighLevel CRM

We use GoHighLevel as our customer relationship management platform. Information submitted through our website forms may be stored and processed within GoHighLevel in order to manage communications and follow-ups.

GoHighLevel's privacy policy is available at www.gohighlevel.com.

Google Ads & Facebook Ads

We run advertising campaigns on Google and Facebook/Meta. These platforms may use cookies and tracking pixels on our website to show you relevant advertisements based on your browsing activity.

You can manage your advertising preferences at any time through Google Ad Settings and Facebook Ad Preferences.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and to track website performance. Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our site.

By continuing to use our website you consent to our use of cookies. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of the site.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as is necessary to provide our services, manage our business relationship with you, or as required by applicable law. When your data is no longer required it will be securely deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to or restrict certain processing of your data.

To make a data request, please contact us at info@csae.ie. We will respond within 30 days.

Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal data, please get in touch:
