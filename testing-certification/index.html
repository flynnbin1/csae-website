Cork Solar & Electrical provides a full range of electrical testing and certification services to homeowners, landlords, businesses and property sellers across Cork City and County Cork. Simple Process

How It Works

Getting your electrical inspection or certificate issued with CSAE is straightforward from first call to delivery.

1

Call or Request a Quote

Get in touch by phone or through our contact form. Tell us what certificate or test you need and we'll arrange a convenient time to visit your property.

2

We Carry Out the Inspection

One of our qualified electricians carries out a thorough inspection or test of your electrical installation, checking all circuits, safety devices and wiring against current ETCI standards.

3

Report or Certificate Issued

We issue your inspection report or certificate promptly on completion — fully compliant and accepted by banks, solicitors, local authorities and insurance companies.
